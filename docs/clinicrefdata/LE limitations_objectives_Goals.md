LE limitations/objectives/Goals

### 

### **Mobility & Transfers**

### **Walking (level/uneven surfaces):**

* ### Unable to walk ¼ mile without onset of leg pain 

* ### Unable to walk on uneven surfaces for \>10 minutes without limping 

* ### Unable to walk 500 feet without rest or assistive device 

* ### Unable to walk 5 minutes at community pace without deviation 

* ### Unable to walk across parking lot while carrying an item 

* ### Unable to walk around a grocery store (10–15 minutes) without stopping 

### **Climbing stairs or ramps:**

* ### Unable to ascend or descend a flight of stairs reciprocally without using handrails 

* ### Unable to climb a ramp without stopping or support 

* ### Unable to ascend 1 flights without increased leg pain 

* ### Unable to climb stairs at normal pace 

* ### Unable to step up 8-inch step without pulling on railing 

### **Descending stairs safely:**

* ### Unable to descend 1–2 flights of stairs with control 

* ### Unable to descend stairs without leading with the same leg 

* ### Unable to descend stairs without relying on handrails 

* ### Unable to perform 10 step-downs without leg fatigue 

* ### Unable to descend stairs while carrying a light object 

### **Sit to stand:**

* ### Unable to rise from chair without pushing off with hands 

* ### Unable to perform 5 sit-to-stands in 30 seconds 

* ### Unable to complete 10 consecutive sit-to-stands without leg pain 

* ### Unable to rise from a low surface without upper body support 

* ### Unable to rise from car seat without compensatory movement 

### **Stepping over obstacles or curbs:**

* ### Unable to step over a 6-inch curb without stumbling or support 

* ### Unable to step over multiple obstacles without dragging foot 

* ### Unable to complete 10 alternating step-overs without stopping 

* ### Unable to clear a 12-inch object without hesitation 

* ### Unable to step over while carrying an object without losing balance 

### **Jumping or hopping:**

* ### Unable to perform 3 consecutive two-footed jumps without pain 

* ### Unable to hop in place on affected leg without loss of balance 

* ### Unable to jump forward 2 feet and land evenly 

* ### Unable to complete lateral hops due to pain or instability 

* ### Unable to complete single-leg hop test with \>90% limb symmetry 

### **Running or jogging:**

* ### Unable to jog ¼ mile without limping or pain 

* ### Unable to run 100–200 feet without altered gait 

* ### Unable to run 1 minute at moderate pace without guarding 

* ### Unable to complete sprint or sport-specific drills 

* ### Unable to run 50 feet without increase in leg symptoms 

### **Changing directions quickly:**

* ### Unable to change direction while walking/jogging without losing balance 

* ### Unable to pivot or side-step without increased pain 

* ### Unable to complete shuttle drill without favoring one leg 

* ### Unable to zig-zag or weave walk/jog without hesitation 

* ### Unable to stop and turn quickly without compensation 

### **Standing for prolonged periods:**

* ### Unable to stand longer than 20 minutes without leaning or shifting weight 

* ### Unable to complete task (e.g., dishwashing) while standing without sitting 

* ### Unable to stand in line for 30 minutes without adjusting position 

* ### Unable to tolerate prolonged standing on hard surfaces 

* ### Unable to maintain upright posture during routine standing tasks 

### **Crawling or kneeling:**

* ### Unable to kneel and return to standing without support 

* ### Unable to crawl 5 feet without sharp knee or hip pain 

* ### Unable to maintain kneeling position for 1 minute 

* ### Unable to assume quadruped position without discomfort 

* ### Unable to bear weight on knees for \>30 seconds 

### **Getting in and out of bed:**

* ### Unable to get in/out of bed without use of hands or assistance 

* ### Unable to swing legs in/out of bed without leg pain 

* ### Unable to transfer in/out of bed without pulling on objects 

* ### Unable to roll and sit up independently 

* ### Unable to exit low bed without pushing with arms 

### **Getting on and off the toilet:**

* ### Unable to sit or rise from toilet without hand support 

* ### Unable to complete toilet transfer without pain increase 

* ### Unable to perform hygiene and clothing tasks independently 

* ### Unable to tolerate low toilet seat without compensation
