List of functional outcome measures

###  **Neck / Cervical Spine**

* **NDI** – Neck Disability Index

* **PSFS** – Patient-Specific Functional Scale

* **VAS/NPRS** – Pain Rating Scales

---

###  **Shoulder**

* **DASH** – Disabilities of the Arm, Shoulder, and Hand

* **QuickDASH** – Shortened version of DASH

* **SPADI** – Shoulder Pain and Disability Index

* **ASES** – American Shoulder and Elbow Surgeons Score

---

###  **Elbow / Wrist / Hand**

* **DASH** / **QuickDASH**

* **PRWE** – Patient-Rated Wrist Evaluation

* **Michigan Hand Outcomes Questionnaire**

---

###  **Thoracic Spine / Mid-back**

* Less region-specific tools; often use:

  * **PSFS**

  * **ODI** if overlapping with low back pain

  * **VAS** for pain

---

###  **Lumbar Spine / Low Back**

* **ODI** – Oswestry Disability Index

* **Roland-Morris Disability Questionnaire**

* **PSFS**

* **FABQ** – Fear-Avoidance Beliefs Questionnaire (if psychosocial impact suspected)

---

###  **Hip**

* **LEFS** – Lower Extremity Functional Scale

* **HOOS** – Hip disability and Osteoarthritis Outcome Score

* **Harris Hip Score** (mostly used post-surgical)

* **PSFS**

---

###  **Knee**

* **LEFS**

* **KOOS** – Knee Injury and Osteoarthritis Outcome Score

* **IKDC** – International Knee Documentation Committee form

* **Lysholm Knee Score**

* **Tegner Activity Scale**

---

###  **Ankle / Foot**

* **FAAM** – Foot and Ankle Ability Measure

* **LEFS**

* **AOFAS** – American Orthopaedic Foot & Ankle Society Score

---

###  **General Mobility / Balance / Falls**

* **BBS** – Berg Balance Scale

* **TUG** – Timed Up and Go

* **5xSTS** – Five Times Sit to Stand

* **DGI** – Dynamic Gait Index

* **FGA** – Functional Gait Assessment

* **ABC** – Activities-specific Balance Confidence Scale

* **Mini-BESTest**

---

###  **Geriatrics / Deconditioning / General Function**

* **LEFS** / **PSFS** (for patient-specific goals)

* **AM-PAC "6-Clicks"**

* **Barthel Index**

* **Functional Independence Measure (FIM)**

* **PROMIS** Measures

---

 **Whole Body / Activity Specific**

* **SMFA** – Short Musculoskeletal Function Assessment

* **SF-36** / **SF-12** – General Health Surveys

* **PSFS** – Useful across all conditions

* **NPRS/VAS** – For subjective pain reporting
